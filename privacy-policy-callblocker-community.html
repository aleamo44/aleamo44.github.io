Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2025

Call Blocker Community (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Spam Reports: Phone numbers you report as spam and the category you assign (e.g., “Telemarketing,” “Fraud”).
  • Problem Reports: If you submit a support request, we collect your email address and issue description to contact you.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous User ID: Generated via Firebase Authentication (anonymous sign-in) to associate reports and logs without requiring personal data.
  • Call and Contact Information (with permission): The App needs access to your call history (numbers, dates, times) and checks numbers against your contacts to avoid blocking them. Your contact list is never uploaded.
  • Advertising Data: Collected via Google AdMob (advertising ID and technical information) for personalized ads.
  • Network Information: Your IP address and network status may be collected by Firebase and AdMob.

C. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, address book, geographic location, or any other sensitive personal data.

2. How Data Is Used and Stored

Data is used to identify and block spam, improve our service, support ads, and respond to support requests.

  • Stored locally: cache, whitelist, settings.
  • Stored securely on Firebase Firestore servers: spam database, your reports.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit, and rely on Google’s secure cloud infrastructure.

4. Third-Party Services & Tracking

We use:

  • Firebase
  • Google Ads

AdMob may use cookies or device identifiers for ad personalization.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Manage reports and whitelist directly in the App.
  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Request deletion of your data via the “Report a Problem” feature.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be communicated through App releases.

8. Contact Us

For questions, please use the “Report a Problem” feature in the App.
