Privacy Policy - HydraTrack

Last Updated: October 2025

HydraTrack ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

All personal and usage data you provide is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. This includes:

  • Profile Information: gender, date of birth, weight, height, activity level, climate setting, and daily routine details (e.g., wake time, meal times, sleep)
  • App Preferences: daily hydration goal, preferred units of measurement, reminder settings, theme, and language
  • Usage Data: history of water intake (amount and timestamps)
  • Gamification Data: daily check-ins with timestamps, points earned (10 points per 100ml water, 10 base points per check-in, bonus points for streaks), badge progress and achievements (13 different badges for streaks, points, and special accomplishments)
  • Smart Features Data: Smart Skip pause durations, Weekend Mode preferences, Stop on Goal settings, and retention tracking (days inactive, current streaks)
  • Analytics Data: calculated statistics, trends, and insights generated from your usage patterns for personalized recommendations

B. Data Sent Externally

Your data may be transmitted outside the App only in these cases:

Contact Us

If you choose to email us via the built-in feature, your message is handled by your own email provider. We do not intercept or store these communications.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses the Google AdMob SDK to display ads. AdMob may collect data such as:

  • Your device's advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Usage information

For more details, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports and diagnostic data when the App unexpectedly terminates. This data includes:

  • Device information (model, manufacturer, Android version)
  • Error stack traces (technical information about the error)
  • App state at the time of the crash
  • Crash timestamps
  • Anonymous installation identifiers (to group crashes from the same installation)

This data is anonymous and is used exclusively to identify and fix technical issues, improving the App's stability. The data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. Permissions Required

The App requests the following Android permissions to function:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send hydration reminders and retention notifications
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To restore reminders after device restart
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: To schedule reminders at precise times
  • INTERNET: Required by AdMob for downloading and displaying ads, and by Firebase Crashlytics for sending crash reports

3. How We Use Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Track your water intake and hydration goals
  • Provide reminders, notifications, and retention re-engagement messages
  • Enable gamification features including points, badges, and achievements
  • Implement smart features like Smart Skip, Weekend Mode, and Stop on Goal
  • Generate personalized analytics, insights, and progress tracking
  • Allow customization of your app experience
  • Display advertising through Google AdMob
  • Identify and fix technical issues through crash reports

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

4. Data Security

All hydration, gamification, analytics, and personal data remain on your device. While we implement reasonable measures, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for safeguarding your device.

Crash reports and diagnostic data transmitted to Firebase are sent over secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS).

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements
  • Firebase Crashlytics - for crash reporting and diagnostics

Their use of your information is governed by their own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Ads: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

7. Your Choices

  • You may modify or delete your data directly within the App
  • You may disable reminders, smart features, and change preferences at any time
  • You may opt out of personalized ads via your device's Google settings
  • You may reset your gamification progress (points, badges, streaks) at any time
  • Crash reporting data is collected automatically and cannot be opted out of while using the App

8. Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained on your device until you delete the App or clear its data
  • Crash reports: Retained by Firebase for up to 90 days for diagnostic purposes
  • Advertising data: Managed according to Google AdMob's retention policies

9. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access: Request information about data collected
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data (done locally in the App)
  • Right to erasure: Delete your data (uninstall the App)
  • Right to data portability: Export your data (done locally via Android backup)

Legal basis for processing:

  • Crash reports: Legitimate interest (improving app stability)
  • Advertising: Consent (can be opted out via Google settings)

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

For questions or concerns, please contact us via the dedicated section in the App (Settings → Contact Us).
